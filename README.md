# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
The E-Commerce Sales Dashboard is a data visualization project built to monitor and analyze key business metrics for an online store. It converts raw sales data into clear insights that help identify top regions, profitable sub-categories, and seasonal trends. The dashboard is designed to support faster, data-driven business decisions.

---

## Key Features & Visualizations

### Executive Summary KPIs
- Total Sales Amount: **438K**  
- Total Profit: **37K**  
- Quantity Sold: **5615**  
- Average Order Value: **121K**

### Geographic Analysis
- Sales breakdown by state  
- **Maharashtra** and **Madhya Pradesh** identified as leading regions

### Customer Insights
- Top-spending customers highlighted through bar chart analysis  
- Examples include **Harivansh** and **Madhav**

### Product Performance
- Profit analysis by sub-category such as **Printers** and **Bookcases**  
- Sales quantity by category showing **Clothing** leading at **63%**

### Payment & Monthly Trends
- Payment mode distribution with **Cash on Delivery (COD)** at **44%**  
- Monthly profit trend to identify seasonal performance patterns

---

## Business Impact
This dashboard helps businesses optimize inventory for high-demand categories like Clothing, focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions, and refine financial planning by understanding profit trends and customer payment behavior.
